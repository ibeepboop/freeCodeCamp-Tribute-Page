Kevin's Famous Chili: A Tribute

by Michelle Meyer

The trick is to undercook the onions. Everyone is going to get to know each other in the pot. -- Kevin Malone

Kevin makes his Famous Chili at least once a year. He stays up the night before to prepare it. Steps include:

  • Pressing garlic
  • Dicing whole tomatoes
  • Toasting his own ancho chiles
